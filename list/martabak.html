Martabak

Source: Nomadette

Ingredients

  • 225g Plain Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 30g Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 325ml Water
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking soda + 1-2 tbsps Water
  • Extra Sugar for Sprinkling

Toppings

  • 2 tbsps Nutella
  • 2 tbsps Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 tbsps Condensed Milk

Steps

  1. Whisk together egg, sugar and vanilla essence until well incorporated.
  2. Sift in flour and add salt. Gradually add water and whisk well.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 1 to 2 hours. You can prepare this batter beforehand. Keep in the fridge if you want to keep it for longer.
  4. 1 to 2 hours later, there should be some bubbles forming in the batter. Prepare baking soda and water mixture. Gently stir into the batter.
  5. Let non-stick pan get hot on medium heat. Make sure pan is hot enough by splashing some water. Water should evaporate immediately. Do not let it get too hot â€“ there should not be smoke rising in pan.
  6. Once pan is hot, pouring in batter. Swivel pan to form a crust.
  7. Leave it alone, and let holes develop.
  8. Once pancake has lots of holes, but the batter is still wet on top, lower the heat. Sprinkle sugar on top.
  9. Cover the pan with a lid, so the steam can finish cooking the top of the pancake.
  10. Pancake is done once the top is fully cooked and there is no wet batter.
  11. Pancake should slide out easily. Slather with butter, and cut in half.
  12. Spread on toppings of choice and top with condensed milk.
  13. Flip one side of the pancake so it forms a sandwich.
  14. Pancake done!
